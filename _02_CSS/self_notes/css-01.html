Poem

Foam, abundant foam on breaking waves Everyone has seen it Not everyone has let the beauty of it in The wonderful orchestra of birds in the morning Everyone has heard it Not everyone has let the beauty of it in The amazing shapes and forms of clouds in the sky Every one has seen them Not everyone has let the beauty of it in. You could easily add a thousand beautiful things We have all seen or heard But we did not take the time to let the beauty in Let us let in every thing of beauty we encounter. Let it touch our inner mind Let it touch our heart and the deepest of our soul.
